Hi, my name is Laurann!

I am currently a Senior at San Jose State University pursuing my bachelors in Design Studies with an emphasis in Graphic Design.

My design style is minimalistic but I love incorporating pops of color to make it stand out. I am inspired by companies such as Apple, who make

bold statements with their artwork, yet it is still simple. On the side, I love taking photographs of people and landscapes and finding unique

places to take photos and finding beauty in every place.
